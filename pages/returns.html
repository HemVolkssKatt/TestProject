Our Return Policy

📦 30-Day Return Window

We want you to love your Furniro furniture. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

  • Items must be in original condition — unused, unassembled, and in the original packaging
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt) is required
  • Custom or made-to-order items are non-returnable

🔄 How to Return

1

Contact Us

Email us at returns@furniro.com or call our support line with your order number.

2

Get Approval

Our team will review your request and provide a Return Authorization (RA) number within 24 hours.

3

Ship It Back

Pack the item securely and ship it to the address provided. We'll cover return shipping on defective items.

4

Get Refunded

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

💰 Refund Details

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • For exchanges, the replacement ships within 3–5 business days after we receive your return
  • Partial refunds may be granted for items not in original condition

⚠️ Damaged or Defective Items

If your furniture arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund — no return shipping required.

Have questions about a return? Our team is here to help.
